What is Neighborhood Swatch?

Neighborhood Swatch is an app created for people who are looking to relocate and purchase a home in the Atlanta area.

Who is the app for?

This app was created with first-time home buyers in mind. While any user could benefit, those unfamiliar with Atlanta and its housing market will have the most to gain from our data.
